Privacy Policy for SmartGPS Camera

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

SmartGPS Camera ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: SmartGPS Camera does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All data processing happens locally on your device.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't do:

  • We do NOT collect your personal information
  • We do NOT store your location data on any servers
  • We do NOT upload your photos to the cloud
  • We do NOT track your usage or behavior
  • We do NOT share data with third parties
  • We do NOT use analytics or advertising services
  • We do NOT require account registration

2. Permissions We Request and Why

Our app requires certain permissions to function properly. Here's what we need and why:

Camera Permission

  • Purpose: To capture photos
  • Usage: Photos are processed and saved locally on your device only
  • Data Storage: All photos remain on your device; we never upload them

Location Permission (GPS)

  • Purpose: To add location information (coordinates, address, altitude, speed) to your photos
  • Usage: Location data is read only when you take a photo and is embedded directly into the photo metadata
  • Data Storage: Location information stays on your device and is only embedded in photos you choose to capture
  • No Tracking: We do not track your location in the background or when the app is not in use

Storage Permission

  • Purpose: To save captured photos to your device's gallery
  • Usage: Photos are saved to your local device storage only

Internet Permission

  • Purpose: To convert GPS coordinates to readable addresses (reverse geocoding)
  • Usage: Only used to fetch address information from geocoding services when taking a photo
  • No Data Collection: We do not send any personal information beyond coordinates needed for address lookup

3. How Your Data is Processed

All data processing happens entirely on your device:

  • Photos: Captured and stored locally in your device's gallery
  • Location Data: Retrieved from your device's GPS and embedded into photo metadata
  • Settings: App preferences are stored locally on your device using shared preferences
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not use any cloud services to store your data

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services for core functionality:

  • Geocoding Services: To convert GPS coordinates to human-readable addresses. Only coordinates are sent; no personal information is transmitted.

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

  • Google Maps Platform: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, our app is safe for users of all ages. However, we recommend parental supervision for children under 13.

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your photos and location data are protected by your device's security measures
  • We recommend using device encryption and lock screen security
  • You have full control over your data and can delete photos at any time from your device's gallery

7. Your Control and Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Permission Control: You can revoke camera, location, or storage permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Data Deletion: Delete any photos captured by the app from your device's gallery
  • App Removal: Uninstalling the app will remove all app-related settings; your photos will remain in your gallery unless you delete them

8. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. When you choose to share photos using your device's share functionality:

  • Sharing is entirely under your control
  • Photos are shared directly from your device to the service/person you select
  • We are not involved in the sharing process beyond providing the share interface

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Compliance

This app is designed to comply with:

  • Google Play Store User Data policies
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) principles
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
